EP-101 · Interior

Interior Painting

Living rooms, kitchens, bedrooms, ceilings, doors, and trim. We move and cover furniture, mask floors and fixtures, repair nail holes and drywall dings, and cut crisp lines at every edge. Low-odor, quality products — and a home you can live in while we work.

EP-102 · Exterior

Exterior Painting

Rim Country weather is hard on a home — intense sun, freezing winters, and monsoon rain. We wash, scrape, sand, caulk, and prime before a drop of finish paint goes on, then use exterior products rated for Arizona's swings so the color holds year after year.

EP-301 · Repaints

Repaints

Refreshing a lived-in home takes more care than painting an empty one. We work room by room, keep dust and disruption down, and match or update colors so the whole house feels intentional — not patched.

EP-302 · New Builds

New Construction

First coats on new custom homes and additions, coordinated with your builder's schedule. Clean surfaces, correct primers, and finishes applied right the first time — so there's nothing to redo later.

EP-401 · Floors

Garage & Shop Epoxy Flooring

Seamless, chemical-resistant epoxy floors that shrug off oil, tires, and dropped tools — and clean up with a hose. A weekend upgrade that makes the garage the best-looking room in the house.
